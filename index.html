NCSU Chess Club September Stalemate

Come join us for the NCSU September Stalemate! This chess tournament will take place on NCSU campus, at 111 Lampe Drive, Room 371 on September 21, 2024. This tournament is open to anyone over the age of 18!

Entries

Event Details:

  • Eligibility: 18+ with a USCF Membership
  • Sections: Sections will be determined based on rating.
  • Round times: 10 am, 1:15 pm, 3:30 pm
  • Time Control: G60, d5
  • Entry fee: $25, payable by Venmo @ncsuchess
  • Prizes: 50% payout
  • Byes: Players are permitted to take one half-point bye. Any subsequent byes will be zero points. Must be requested before Round 1 starts. Round 3 byes are automatically zero points.

Please fill out this form to sign up: https://tinyurl.com/y93du2hs

No entries will be accepted after 9:30am on September 21.

We encourage players to bring their own boards, sets, and clocks. Notation materials will be provided. Email sasampa2@ncsu.edu with any further questions!
