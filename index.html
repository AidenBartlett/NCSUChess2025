NCSU Chess Club April Gambit

Come join us for the NCSU April Gambit! This chess tournament will take place on NCSU campus, at Talley Student Union Room 4280 on April 13, 2025. This tournament is open to anyone over the age of 18!

Entries

Event Details:

  • Eligibility: 18+ with a USCF Membership
  • Sections: Sections will be determined based on rating.
  • Round times: 10 am, 1:15 pm, 3:30 pm
  • Time Control: G60, d5
  • Entry fee: $25, payable by Venmo @ncsuchess
  • Prizes: 50% payout
  • Byes: Players are permitted to take one half-point bye. Any subsequent byes will be zero points. Must be requested before Round 1 starts. Round 3 byes are automatically zero points.

Please fill out this form to sign up: https://tinyurl.com/236mf62n

No entries will be accepted after 9:30am on April 13.

We encourage players to bring their own boards, sets, and clocks. Notation materials will be provided. Email chessteam.ncsu@gmail.com with any further questions!
